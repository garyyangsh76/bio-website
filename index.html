What We Do

A medical-grade silicone platform built for demanding applications.

BIO Corporation is the authorized regional distributor of NuSil by Avantor — the global standard for implantable and medical-grade silicone. We supply materials, provide formulation support, and connect engineering teams with the technical depth they need to qualify and scale.

Product Platform

Structured for clarity, engineered for expansion.

Four core material families covering the full spectrum of medical silicone requirements — from long-term implants to precision device assembly.

Implant-Grade Silicone

High-purity elastomers, gels, and adhesives for long-term in-vivo performance. Qualified to ISO 10993 and USP Class VI for cardiac, neural, orthopedic, and soft-tissue implants.

Request Sample →

Medical Adhesives

One- and two-component RTV systems for device bonding, PCB encapsulation, skin-contact interfaces, and catheter tipping. Flexible cure schedules and substrate compatibility.

Request Sample →

LSR / HCR Elastomers

Liquid silicone rubber and high-consistency rubber for injection molding, transfer molding, and extrusion. Broad durometer range with custom compounding options.

Fluorosilicone Systems

Specialty silicones with outstanding resistance to fuels, solvents, and aggressive media. For applications where standard silicone performance is insufficient.

Applications

Precision materials across the medical device spectrum.

Medical Devices

Implantable components, catheter bodies, drug delivery coatings, wound care interfaces, and surgical instrument grips requiring long-term biocompatibility.

Wearables & Sensing

Skin-contact enclosures, hearable devices, CGM patches, and flexible sensor substrates demanding durability, biocompatibility, and process consistency.

Aesthetic & Reconstructive

Breast implant shells and fill gels, facial reconstruction, and injectable-grade systems requiring the highest purity and complete regulatory documentation.

Optical & Precision

Intraocular lens materials, ophthalmic bonding adhesives, and precision optical encapsulants with defined refractive and clarity properties.
